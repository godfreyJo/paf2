How Our Programs Work

Education

Education Access Program

We provide essential learning materials including textbooks, notebooks, pencils, and school uniforms to children who would otherwise miss school due to lack of supplies.

  • Textbook distribution to partner schools
  • Stationery kits for every student
  • School uniform assistance
Empowerment

Girl Child Support

Our girl child program addresses the unique challenges that prevent girls from attending school, providing sanitary products, mentorship, and a supportive community.

  • Monthly sanitary pad distribution
  • Female mentorship programs
  • Life skills workshops
Health

Health & Hygiene

Good health is essential for learning. Our hygiene program teaches proper hygiene practices and provides personal care essentials to keep children healthy and in school.

  • Hygiene education workshops
  • Personal care essentials distribution
  • Undergarment support
Community

Community Outreach

We believe in working together with communities. Our outreach programs engage local leaders, schools, and families to create sustainable change.

  • Community events and workshops
  • Volunteer drives
  • Local partnerships in Kakamega
