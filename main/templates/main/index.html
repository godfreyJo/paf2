About Us

Phidelis Ann Foundation (PAF) is a community-driven organization committed to improving access to education and restoring dignity among learners in underserved communities across Kenya. We believe that every child deserves a fair chance to learn, grow, and achieve their full potential—regardless of their background.

Read Our Story

Our Mission

To empower children and youth by providing essential educational and personal support, enabling them to stay in school, learn effectively, and thrive with dignity.

Our Vision

A society where no child is held back from education due to lack of basic needs, and where every learner is equipped with the tools and confidence to build a brighter future.

Why Our Work Matters

In many parts of Kenya, students miss school not because they lack ability, but because they lack basic necessities. Something as simple as a school uniform or sanitary pad can determine whether a child attends class or stays at home. At Phidelis Ann Foundation, we are committed to closing this gap—one child, one school, and one community at a time.
