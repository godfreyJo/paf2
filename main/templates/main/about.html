Our Story

Phidelis Ann Foundation was founded in 2023 with a simple yet powerful belief: every child deserves access to education and the dignity to learn without barriers.

Named after our founder, Phidelis Ann who dedicated her life to educating children in her community, PAF continues that legacy of compassion and commitment to education.

What started as a small initiative in Kakamega has grown into a movement that has touched the lives of over 200 children across 5 partner schools and 8 communities.

Our work focuses on four key areas: Education Access, Girl Child Support, Community Outreach, and Health & Hygiene. Through these programs, we address the root causes that keep children out of school.
